Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At The Best Converter, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

The Best Converter operates entirely in your web browser. We want to be clear about what this means:

  • Files You Convert: All file processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are NEVER uploaded to our servers.
  • Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, session duration, etc.). This data is anonymized.

Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements on our website. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your browsing history.
  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand website traffic and user behavior.

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they use information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our service without creating an account
  • Clear your browser cookies at any time
  • Disable JavaScript to prevent analytics (though this will break the converter functionality)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your files or personal information, there's no data to be breached. All processing happens in your browser, providing maximum security and privacy.

Children's Privacy

Our service is available to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our contact page.
