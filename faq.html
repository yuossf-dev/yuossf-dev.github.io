General Questions

Is The Best Converter really free?

Yes, absolutely! All our conversion tools are 100% free to use with no hidden charges, subscriptions, or premium tiers. We believe file conversion should be accessible to everyone. Our service is supported by non-intrusive advertisements, which allow us to keep the tool free forever.

Do I need to create an account?

No! You can use all our tools without any registration or sign-up. We don't collect personal information, track your usage patterns, or require email addresses. Simply visit the site and start converting immediately.

How many files can I convert?

There's no limit! Convert as many files as you need, whenever you need. You can also batch process multiple files at once. Since everything runs in your browser, you can convert hundreds or thousands of files without any restrictions.

How long does conversion take?

Conversions are usually instant! Small images convert in milliseconds, while larger files (10MB+) may take a few seconds. Since all processing happens locally in your browser, there's no upload/download time. The speed depends mainly on your device's processing power and the file size.

File Formats & Conversion

What file formats do you support?

We support a wide range of formats:

  • Images: JPG/JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, TIFF, HEIC, SVG
  • Documents: PDF creation from images and text
  • Image Operations: Resize, compress, rotate, flip, crop

We're constantly adding support for more formats. Check our blog for updates!

Should I use JPG or PNG for my images?

It depends on your needs:

Use JPG for:

  • Photographs and realistic images
  • When file size is more important than perfect quality
  • Social media posts and email attachments

Use PNG for:

  • Logos and graphics with transparent backgrounds
  • Screenshots and images with text
  • When you need perfect quality without compression

Read our detailed guide: JPG vs PNG

How much can I compress my images?

Compression rates vary by image type. Typically, you can reduce file sizes by 50-80% while maintaining good visual quality. Photos often compress better than graphics. Our tool gives you control over quality settings, allowing you to find the perfect balance between file size and image quality for your specific needs.

Can I convert multiple images to one PDF?

Yes! Our Image to PDF tool allows you to select multiple images and merge them into a single PDF document. You can reorder the images before conversion, and the resulting PDF will contain all your images as separate pages. Perfect for creating photo albums, presentations, or documentation.

Technical Questions

Is there a file size limit?

There are no artificial limits imposed by us. The only limitation is your browser's available memory. Modern browsers can typically handle files up to several hundred megabytes. Very large files (500MB+) may cause performance issues depending on your device's RAM. For best performance, we recommend processing files under 100MB.

Which browsers are supported?

The Best Converter works on all modern browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended) - Version 90+
  • Mozilla Firefox - Version 88+
  • Microsoft Edge - Version 90+
  • Safari (Mac/iOS) - Version 14+
  • Opera - Version 76+

For best performance, keep your browser updated to the latest version.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes! Our converter is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and all mobile devices. The interface adapts to your screen size for optimal usability. However, processing large files on mobile devices may be slower due to limited processing power. For best results with large files, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer.

Can I use it offline?

Once the page is loaded, basic conversions work offline! Since all processing is client-side, you can convert files even without an internet connection (as long as the page is already loaded in your browser). However, you'll need internet to initially load the page and access external resources like fonts and icons.

Why is conversion so fast?

Our converter runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas API. There's no uploading files to a server, waiting in queue, or downloading results. Everything happens instantly on your device, which means conversions are as fast as your computer can process them - usually milliseconds for small files!

Privacy & Security

Are my files safe?

Absolutely! Your files are 100% safe. All conversions happen entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server. Once you close the page, all data is immediately deleted from your browser's memory. We have zero access to your files.

Do you store my converted files?

No, never. Since all processing happens in your browser, your files don't even reach our servers. There's nothing for us to store. Your original files and converted results exist only in your browser's memory during the conversion process, and are automatically cleared when you close the tab or browser.

Can I convert confidential documents safely?

Yes! Our client-side processing makes it perfect for confidential documents. Since nothing is uploaded to the internet, your sensitive files remain completely private. This is ideal for converting personal photos, business documents, financial records, or any other private information. No one - including us - can access your files.

What data do you collect?

  • Browser type and device information (for compatibility)
  • General usage patterns (to improve the tool)

We DO NOT collect:

  • Your files or file contents
  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • File names or metadata

See our Privacy Policy for details.
